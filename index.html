RULES

1. In Blackjack, the goal is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over, and having a higher total than the dealer. If you bust (exceed 21), you lose. The dealer also plays, and their hand is revealed after all players have made their decisions.

2. Card Values: Cards 2-9 have their face value. 10, Jack, Queen, and King are worth 10. An Ace can be 1 or 11, whichever is most beneficial to the player.

3. Initial Deal:The dealer deals each player two cards face up, and deals themselves two cards, one face up and one face down.

4. Player's Turn: Players decide whether to "hit" (take another card) or "stand" (stop taking cards).If a player busts, they automatically lose.

5. Dealer's Turn:After all players have made their decisions, the dealer reveals their face-down card. The dealer follows specific rules for hitting or standing (usually outlined by the casino).

6. Payouts:Player Wins: If a player's hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's, they win. Dealer Busts: If the dealer busts, all players who haven't busted win. Push: If the player and dealer have the same total, it's a push (tie), and the bet is returned.
